Common Home App

The Common Home app is currently under construction. If you'd like to be one of the first to try it our then please get in touch.

The app is a key part of a wider service to make Commonhold easy and simple for all. The app helps guide individuals who are leading their change in their buildings through each step of connecting, communicating and delivering on Commonhold. It also provides a way of those are joining in to get involved, helping them keep up to date with progress and take on responsibilities for their homes.

Who we are

Common Home is a social enterprise. It began life as an innovation project from Unboxed. It is an idea by Tom Gayler, a designer who has designed and built services across government, the NHS and private sector.
